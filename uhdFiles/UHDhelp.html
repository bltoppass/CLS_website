Computer Science External Help Page




This website contains links to easy-to-understand information that’s tailored to the Computer Science courses at UHD. The chapters of the in-class textbook are broken down into sections, each one filled with a 10-15 minute video that can help students grasp the gist of the subject at hand.

Professors forget what it’s like to be a beginner because they’re professionals. And so, what may seem like a very simple explanation from their perspective ends up being technical jargon to the novices. The knowledge presented here is done by amateurs who can explain things so well and simple, even a 5th grader could understand. This makes learning enjoyable and school life much more easier to manage.
